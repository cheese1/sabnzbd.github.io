--- title: Contact ---

Overview

Development of SABnzbd is hosted on GitHub.com
Translations are managed on LaunchPad.

User support

Questions, answers and bug reports can be found on the support forum
(For posting messages, we require you to register on the forum, to keep the spammers out.)
You can see unread forum messages via https://forums.sabnzbd.org/search.php?search_id=unreadposts
Recent news is also shown on http://sabnzbd.org

IRC channel

Server: irc.synirc.net port 6668 (7001 for SSL)
Channel: #sabnzbd
Web Interface: http://sabnzbd.org/live-chat

Bug reporting

Always report bugs on the Forum and not in emails or Forum PM's.
You may be asked to send additional material to bugs@sabnzbd.org.
Do not send unsolicited information to our email addresses, it will be ignored.
When you send information always add the URL of your forum post.
If you have a GitHub account you can submit problem reports and change requests in our bugtracker.
