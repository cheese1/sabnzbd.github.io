Free and easy binary newsreader

It runs anywhere

SABnzbd runs on Windows, MacOS, Linux. And also on Unix, BSD, certain NAS devices (Synology, QNAP). You can access SABnzbd from any device with a webbrowser, so also iOS and Android phones and tablets.

Small and large screens

SABnzbd is responsive: With the Glitter interface, you can access SABnzbd and manage your downloads from any device: desktop, tablet or phone. Others have made Android (nzb360) and iOS (SABmini) apps to manage SABnzbd.

Integrates with other tools

Apps like Sonarr, Sickrage, CouchPotato and Headphones can integrate with SABnzbd and automate your download process.
SABnzbd can also read and process RSS feeds, activate custom scripts and notify you via mail, growl, prowl, pushover and pushbullet.

In 15 languages

SABnzbd now ships with 15 localizations:
English, Dansk, Deutsch, Español (castellano), Français, Nederlands, Norsk bokmål, Polski, Português Brasileiro, Româna, Suomi, Svenska, русский язык, српски, 简体中文.
